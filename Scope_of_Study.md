Scope of the Study

This study focuses on understanding the role of Artificial Intelligence in enhancing business analytics and supporting organizational decision-making.

The research adopts a systematic literature review approach and examines existing studies related to Artificial Intelligence and Business Analytics.

Areas Covered

- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Natural Language Processing
- Generative AI
- Descriptive Analytics
- Predictive Analytics
- Prescriptive Analytics

Industry Applications

- Marketing
- Finance
- Healthcare
- Supply Chain Management
- Customer Analytics

Key Issues

- Data Privacy
- Ethical Concerns
- Explainability
- Bias and Fairness
- Implementation Challenges

The study is limited to secondary data obtained from academic journals, conference papers, books, and industry reports.
