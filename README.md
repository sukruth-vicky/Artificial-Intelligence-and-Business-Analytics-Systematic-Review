The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Enhancing Business Analytics and Decision-Making: A Systematic Review

Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative technology influencing how organizations analyze data and make strategic decisions. This research project aims to explore the role of AI in enhancing business analytics and improving organizational decision-making through a systematic review of existing literature.

The study investigates how AI technologies such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Generative AI are reshaping business analytics across industries.


Research Objectives

* Examine the relationship between Artificial Intelligence and Business Analytics.
* Analyze the impact of AI on organizational decision-making.
* Identify major applications of AI-driven analytics.
* Investigate the opportunities and challenges associated with AI adoption.
* Explore future research directions in AI-enabled business analytics.
