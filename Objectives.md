General Objective

To examine the role of Artificial Intelligence in enhancing business analytics and organizational decision-making through a systematic review of existing literature.

Specific Objectives
To analyze the relationship between Artificial Intelligence and Business Analytics.
To examine how AI technologies improve organizational decision-making.
To identify major applications of AI-driven business analytics across industries.
To investigate the opportunities and challenges associated with AI adoption.
To explore emerging trends and future research directions in AI-enabled business analytics.
