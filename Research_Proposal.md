Title

The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Enhancing Business Analytics and Decision-Making: A Systematic Review

Background

The increasing adoption of Artificial Intelligence has significantly transformed the field of business analytics. Organizations are increasingly utilizing AI technologies to process large volumes of data, generate insights, automate processes, and improve decision-making capabilities.

Despite the growing importance of AI, there remains a need to understand how AI contributes to business analytics and what challenges and opportunities arise from its implementation.

Problem Statement

Organizations are investing heavily in Artificial Intelligence technologies; however, the integration of AI with business analytics presents challenges related to ethics, explainability, privacy, and implementation complexity. A comprehensive review is required to understand the role of AI in enhancing business analytics and supporting strategic decision-making.

Purpose of the Study

To systematically review existing literature and examine how Artificial Intelligence contributes to business analytics and organizational decision-making.

Research Method

Systematic Literature Review (SLR)

Expected Contributions
Provide insights into AI-enabled business analytics.
Identify current trends and applications.
Highlight challenges and limitations.
Suggest future research directions.
